E&ICT, AI and Computing Lab

The AI and Computing Lab is a cutting-edge facility designed for advanced research and development in artificial intelligence and computing. It features Dell Precision 7820 tower workstations, each powered by an Intel Xeon Silver 4214 processor and boasting 128 GB of RAM. These workstations support dual-boot with Windows 10 and CentOS 7 for versatility.

The lab offers robust connectivity through LAN and Wi-Fi, fostering efficient collaboration. Software resources include MATLAB, OpenNN, ClearML, Tensor Flow, PyTorch, and OpenCV for AI and machine learning research. Additionally, Synopsys Quantum ATK enables atomic-level materials and device simulation.

Raspberry Pi kits are available for embedded systems and IoT projects, promoting hands-on learning. Workstations are equipped with 24 GB of GPU memory, ECC for error correction, and four display ports for multi-monitor setups, enhancing productivity. This lab is a comprehensive hub for AI, computing, materials science, and quantum mechanics research.
